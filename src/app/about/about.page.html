Why Amandeep Hospital

Amandeep Group of Hospitals is one of the leading healthcare service provider groups of the country. Amandeep Group has come a long way and its journey dates back to 1990 when it came into existence. It has expanded since then and has reached masses to provide the most effective treatments.

The doctors and the staff at Amandeep Group of Hospitals are being led by the aim to save each precious life. It keeps on updating the medical technology and stands out as a citadel of excellence with modern treatments and veteran doctors.

Dr. Amandeep Kaur and Dr. Avtar Singh established Amandeep Hospital and at that time it was a 5-bedded speciality centre for Orthopaedics and Trauma management. Rendering relentless efforts in providing the best clinical care to the patients, Amandeep Hospital evolved into a 500-bedded multispeciality centre for healthcare.

And it does not end here for the group has widened the scope of its healthcare provisions. This group has many branches and associations which are helping it to serve the society with its medical assistance. The branches are:

  • Amandeep Medicity
  • Amandeep Hospital (Pathankot)
  • Amandeep Hospital (Ajnala)
  • OPD clinics in Jammu and Srinagar
  • Amritsar Super Scan facility with 3 TESLA MRI machines which offers matchless services to the patients.

Some associations have acted as the harbinger of better healthcare facilities to the people. These associations are:

  • OPD clinics in Ferozepur
  • OPD in Jalandhar
  • Amandeep Kamal Hospital at Tarn Taran
  • Amandeep - Eden Centre of Excellence for Orthopaedics, Trauma Care & Joint Replacement
  • OPD clinic in Batala

We have Laser treatment for Varicose veins, Hyperbaric Chamber for Oxygen therapy, Navigation technology for Joint Replacement besides other world-class treatments at the hospital facility. It has come up as a group of hospitals which aims to treat all irrespective of any factors and gift healthy life to its patients.
